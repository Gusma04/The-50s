Univac 1

Univac 1 was the first commercial computer created in the United States. It was designed by J. Presper Eckert and John Mauchly, who began developing it within their company, the Eckert-Mauchly Computer Corporation. UNIVAC I was handed over to the United States Census Bureau on March 31, 1951.
